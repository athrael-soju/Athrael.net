Black Desert online: The Forge

Welcome to The Forge: A weapon/armor (and soon an accessory) enchantment simulator which calculates enchant costs in silver, based on user defined parameters.

Take a look below for an estimate on Armor and Weapon enchant success percentages. Please keep in mind that this information was deduced by the community by trial and error and by no means should be taken literally, when enchanting your equipment.

Analysis: Here you can run the simulation up to 100 times. The reason you'd want to do that is to get an idea of the underlying distribution or curve, if any. You can also see the luckiest/unluckiest enchant that results from every set of simulations, which can help you define whether your risk/success rate is worthwhile.
